**Catholic Political Engagement: Integralism vs. Pragmatism**

- In a secular democracy, should a Catholic's goal be the establishment of a "Social Kingship of Christ" (Catholic Integralism), or is it more practical to work within the existing system and vote for the "lesser of two evils"?

**Entertainment: Quarantine vs. Critical Engagement**

- Should a Catholic family completely avoid modern entertainment (movies, music, TV shows) due to its pervasive immorality? Or is it possible and even necessary to critically engage with culture to understand and evangelize it?

**Scrupulosity vs. a Well-Formed Conscience**

- In a community that rightly emphasizes the gravity of sin, how do we avoid falling into the spiritual disease of scrupulosity? What is the difference between holy fear of the Lord and a paralyzing, unhealthy fear?

**The Role of the Laity**

- Beyond the essential duties of Mass, prayer, and raising a family, what is the proper role of the layman and laywoman in the restoration of the Church? Is the primary focus on the family, or is there a call to more public action and apostolate?
